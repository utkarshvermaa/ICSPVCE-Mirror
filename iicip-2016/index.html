Welcome To IICIP 2016

     Information Processing is an emerging area in research due to its application in diverse research areas. The main thrust of Information processing is to implement efficient intelligent systems for enhancing the quality of decision making. Such systems use systematic ways of processing the massive information or heuristic processing for more constructive evaluation of information. India International Conference on Information Processing-2016 is a peer-reviewed scientific conference, stressing on balanced reporting of both theory and practice. The vision of this international conference is the diffusion of interesting knowledge on various subjects intertwined with information processing.
     IICIP encourages submission of high quality, result oriented papers as well as survey and review papers in various domain of information processing. With this conference we construct a foundation for the fulfillment of the most brilliant and promising research in this field.
     IICIP provides a wide platform to facilitate researchers from academia, industry and government organization to share their novel ideas. This conference aims to provide an international forum for contemplating the interdependent and inter-disciplinary research ideas for enhanced quality work on information processing. Thus, we welcome diverse innovative ideas towards the furtherance of this aim.
